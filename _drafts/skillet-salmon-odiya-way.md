Fish is a staple in Odiya household. But Salmon was not our favorite at first. First time it felt so fishy being used to the sweet taste of fresh water fish. But over the years Salmon is an weekly affair. And I have learnt few knacks to please the family with few modifications to the cooking methods we traditionally use back in home. I keep the spices same but use it in a different way so we cherish both the memories of home with the goodness of Salmon.

Here's a crisp skillet Salmon recipe:

#### Ingredients:

1. Salmon - 2 large pieces
2. Mustard Oil - 2 tbsp
3. Spice Rub - 2tbsp
4. Salt - according to taste
5. Turmeric - 2 tsp

Steps of Preparation of the Skillet Salmon Recipe:

1. Marinate the Fish with Spice Rub, Salt and Turmeric for 10-15 mins.
2. Heat a thick bottomed skillet.
3. Once the skillet is heated , add the oil and fry the Salmon until the skin is crisp around (2-3) times and flip and cook the other side until crisp.

Serve as a protein for your tacos or along side Rice and Dal the Odiya way or as a main dish with a side of fresh salad.
