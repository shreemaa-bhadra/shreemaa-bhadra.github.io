Salad is an interesting dish. And summer is definitely for salads. I love multiple variations of Salads. Some salads are sides in my main course and the thumb rule for those salads are cut raw veggies with minimal seasonings. But some Salads are my whole meals and that time I do make sure to enrich the proper balance of nutrients versus the flavors. Some people misunderstand Salad for dieting, but no salads are a proper balance of nutrients and a pack of flavors. I do ensure to enrich my salads with the proper amount of carbs, proteins, fats, and no doubt the power pack of vitamins and minerals.

This Summer Salad is made with a variation of Seafood and all veggies taking into account the vegetarian choice of diet. There is a freshness of summer fresh veggies, loaded with the freshness of lemon. And of course, the main stars are crispy Roti toppings (in place of tortilla chips or croutons) and the vegetarian version has Tandoori Aloo and the seafood version has Tandoori Prawn.
