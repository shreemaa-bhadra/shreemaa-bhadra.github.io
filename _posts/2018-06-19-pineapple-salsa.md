## Ingredients for Pineapple Salsa:

1. Pineapple - 1 cup, finely chopped
2. Red Bell Pepper - 1/4 cup, baked and chopped
3. Hot Jalapenos - 1 , deseeded and finely chopped
4. Coriander Leaves - 1/4 cup, finely chopped.
5. Red Pearl Onions or Indian Shallots - 2 tbsp, finely chopped
6. Garlic - 1 clove, finely grated
7. Roasted Cumin Powder - 2 tsp
8. Paprika Powder - 1 tsp
9. Salt - according to taste
10. Lime Juice - 2 tbsp

## Steps of Pre - Preparation:

1. Finely chop, the veggies as listed in the ingredient list. Make them as small as possible to give that chunky salsa texture.
2. Bake the Red Bell Pepper at 400 F for 40 mins. And then chop it into equal size as other veggies.

## Steps of Preparation:

1. Bring a large mixing bowl, add all the ingredients.
2. Mix all the ingredients so the spices and juices mix uniformly with all the ingredient. Serve it fresh to bring out the best of all the ingredients.
