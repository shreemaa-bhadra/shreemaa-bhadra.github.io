When I was a child, egg was a life savior when fish or meat weren't available. If I wanted something not so veggie, mom would cook this "Anda aloo Bhurji" in a jiffy and serve it with hot chapatis or dal rice. The best thing about this dish is the simplicity, faint flavors of cumin, caramelized onion and my love for Potatoes.

![Anda Aloo Bhurji and rice](/assets/uploads/2018/07/DSC_6575.jpg "Anda Aloo Bhurji and rice")

Anda Aloo Bhurji and rice

I wanted a simple dish for the dinner. The little one wants everything distinct and dry which he can hold, wave and then finally chew with all plays. Anda Aloo Bhurji turned out to be a great success and he munched it without a tantrum.

Here goes the recipe for Anda Aloo Bhurji:

## Ingredients for Anda Aloo Bhurji or Potato and Egg stir fry:

![Ingredients](/assets/uploads/2018/07/DSC_6520.jpg "Ingredients")

Ingredients

1. Eggs -  3 big sized ones
2. Potatoes - 2 medium sized
3. Onion - 1 medium sized
4. Green chilies - 3
5. Coriander Leaves - handful
6. Cumin seeds - 1 tsp
7. Mustard Oil - 2 tbsp
8. Turmeric Powder - 1 tsp
9. Salt - according to taste

## Steps of Preparation of Anda Aloo Bhurji or Potato and Egg stir fry:

1. Cut the Onions and Potatoes into thin slices, almost in equal sizes.
   
![Cut potatoes and onions](/assets/uploads/2018/07/DSC_6528.jpg "Cut potatoes and onions")

Cut potatoes and onions
2. Finely chop the green chilies and coriander leaves.
3. Beat the eggs with a pinch of turmeric and a pinch of salt. Don't overdo salt as we will be adding it to the potatoes too.
4. Heat a frying pan with the oil.
   
![Oil pan](/assets/uploads/2018/07/DSC_6530.jpg "Oil pan")

Oil pan
5. Once heated up, add the green chilies and cumin seeds to the oil and let it splutter for few seconds.
   
![Green chilies and cumin seeds in the oil](/assets/uploads/2018/07/DSC_6537.jpg "Green chilies and cumin seeds in the oil")

Green chilies and cumin seeds in the oil
6. Then add the Onions until it starts caramelizing, and that's where the predominating flavor of the dish gets.
   
![Adding onions](/assets/uploads/2018/07/DSC_6538.jpg "Adding onions")

Adding onions
   
![Caramelizing onions](/assets/uploads/2018/07/DSC_6543.jpg "Caramelizing onions")

Caramelizing onions
7. Add the potatoes. To it add turmeric powder and salt and saute for a minute and then cover and cook for around 3-4 mins, until potatoes are half done.
   
![Adding Potatoes](/assets/uploads/2018/07/DSC_6544.jpg "Adding Potatoes")

Adding Potatoes
   
![Adding turmeric and salt](/assets/uploads/2018/07/DSC_6548.jpg "Adding turmeric and salt")

Adding turmeric and salt
   
![Potatoes, turmeric and salt being mixed](/assets/uploads/2018/07/DSC_6551.jpg "Potatoes, turmeric and salt being mixed")

Potatoes, turmeric and salt being mixed
   
![Covering the mix](/assets/uploads/2018/07/DSC_6555.jpg "Covering the mix")

Covering the mix
   
![Taking out the lid](/assets/uploads/2018/07/DSC_6558.jpg "Taking out the lid")

Taking out the lid
8. To the semi cooked potatoes, add the beaten egg and keep on stirring so that egg does not stick to the bottom or is burnt.
   
![Adding beaten egg](/assets/uploads/2018/07/DSC_6565.jpg "Adding beaten egg")

Adding beaten egg
   
![Stirring the mix](/assets/uploads/2018/07/DSC_6566.jpg "Stirring the mix")

Stirring the mix
9. Cook until the eggs and potatoes are done.
   
![Keep cooking](/assets/uploads/2018/07/DSC_6570.jpg "Keep cooking")

Keep cooking

Garnish with coriander leaves to add freshness of taste.
