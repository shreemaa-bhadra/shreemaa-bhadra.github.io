The festivity and chilly winter in US are gearing me in my baking mood. Meanwhile, my 10 months old infant is keeping me super busy with his fast crawls and babbles. He sometimes grabs my attention to the extent that he doesn't allow me to blink away from him. That's so possessive of him and I probably love it the most now. However, what brought this post of mine is not some planned Christmas massive baking or a party. My husband played for me an absolute masterpiece for food bloggers, "Julie and Julia". This had a sudden urge in me to bake these lovelies. Now, why Biscotti? Why not some other cookies? Cause it's Christmas time and these Biscotti's have stolen my heart and mind ever since I have tasted them this summer. Before that it was just a name which I had heard in many movies. So, here I'm making almond and choco-chip Biscotti.

[![Almond and Choco-chip Biscotti](/assets/uploads/2016/12/DSC_9741.jpg)](/assets/uploads/2016/12/DSC_9741.jpg) Almond and Choco-chip Biscotti

These Italian crisp _Biscotti_ are the double baked cookies which it literally means. The double baked _Biscotti_ are traditionally made out of almonds. I wanted was to desperately bake the chocolate ones, but my pantry missed the cocoa powder. Hence, I dropped the idea and baked the basic ones with almonds and choco-chips.

## **Ingredients:**

[![Almond and Choco-chip Biscotti \_Choco chipAlmond and Choco-chip Biscotti \_Choco chip](/assets/uploads/2016/12/DSC_9681.jpg)](/assets/uploads/2016/12/DSC_9681.jpg) Choco chips

[![Almond and Choco-chip Biscotti \_Chopped Almonds](/assets/uploads/2016/12/DSC_9683.jpg)](/assets/uploads/2016/12/DSC_9683.jpg) Almonds

1. All purpose flour - 2 cups
2. Eggs - 3 large ones
3. Brown sugar - 1 cup
4. Butter (softened)- 1 stick (100 gms)
5. Baking powder - 1 tsp
6. Vanilla extract - 1 tsp
7. Salt - 1tsp
8. Choco-chips - 1 cup
9. Almonds (toasted and roughly chopped) - 1 cup
10. Egg wash - (made out of beating one egg with 2 tbsp of milk)

\[the\_ad id="355"\]

## **Steps of Preparation:**

1. Preheat the oven for 30 mins at a temperature of 180°C or 350 F.
2. There after mix the butter and sugar, until the sugar dissolves properly.
    
    [![Almond and Choco-chip Biscotti \_Step](/assets/uploads/2016/12/DSC_9689.jpg)](/assets/uploads/2016/12/DSC_9689.jpg) Mix the butter and sugar
    
    [![Almond and Choco-chip Biscotti \_Step](/assets/uploads/2016/12/DSC_9692.jpg)](/assets/uploads/2016/12/DSC_9692.jpg) Mix the butter and sugar
3. Beat the eggs and whisk it in the butter sugar mixture.
    
    [![Almond and Choco-chip Biscotti \_Whishking Egg](/assets/uploads/2016/12/DSC_9687.jpg)](/assets/uploads/2016/12/DSC_9687.jpg) Whisking egg
4. Sieve the dry ingredients (flour and baking soda), add the vanilla extract to the whisked mixture and knead a dough similar to the ones you make for cookies.
    
    [![Almond and Choco-chip Biscotti \_Sieved Flour](/assets/uploads/2016/12/DSC_9677.jpg)](/assets/uploads/2016/12/DSC_9677.jpg) Sieved Flour and baking soda
    
    [![Almond and Choco-chip Biscotti \_Step](/assets/uploads/2016/12/DSC_9699.jpg)](/assets/uploads/2016/12/DSC_9699.jpg) Adding vanilla extract
    
    [![Almond and Choco-chip Biscotti \_Step](/assets/uploads/2016/12/DSC_9701.jpg)](/assets/uploads/2016/12/DSC_9701.jpg) Add the butter plus sugar mixture
5. Add the chopped almonds and choco-chips to the dough.
    
    [![Almond and Choco-chip Biscotti \_Step](/assets/uploads/2016/12/DSC_9705.jpg)](/assets/uploads/2016/12/DSC_9705.jpg) Almond and Choco-chip Dough
6. If the rolls appear to sticky, knead a bit more with the dry flour. In a baking tray take two elongated bread rolls shaped dough and brush the rolls with the egg wash, so that the cookie gets a wonderful glaze and let it bake for 25 mins.
    
    [![Almond and Choco-chip Biscotti \_Step](/assets/uploads/2016/12/DSC_9708.jpg)](/assets/uploads/2016/12/DSC_9708.jpg) Kneading with dry flour
    
    [![Almond and Choco-chip Biscotti \_the elongated rolls to be baked](/assets/uploads/2016/12/DSC_9712.jpg)](/assets/uploads/2016/12/DSC_9712.jpg) The elongated rolls to be baked
    
    [![Almond and Choco-chip Biscotti\_Baking the Cookie](/assets/uploads/2016/12/DSC_9717.jpg)](/assets/uploads/2016/12/DSC_9717.jpg) Baking the Cookie
    
    [!["<yoastmark](/assets/uploads/2016/12/DSC_9723.jpg)](/assets/uploads/2016/12/DSC_9723.jpg)
7. Cut the bread roll shaped cookies into the Biscotti form as shown in the image and bake it again for 15-20 mins.
    
    [![Almond and Choco-chip Biscotti \_second round of baking tray after cutting into smaller pieces](/assets/uploads/2016/12/DSC_9724.jpg)](/assets/uploads/2016/12/DSC_9724.jpg) Second round of baking tray after cutting into smaller pieces
    
    [![Almond and Choco-chip Biscotti \_SecondRound of Baking](/assets/uploads/2016/12/DSC_9726.jpg)](/assets/uploads/2016/12/DSC_9726.jpg) Second round of baking

Hurray!!, you have the fresh, crisp Biscotti ready. Simply dunk it in your favorite tea or coffee and enjoy it this Christmas season. I bet you are going to have the best tea time snack for yourself.
