A lazy weekend after five days of bone breaking office weekdays, feels like a pure bliss. The work has taken its toll and both of us are having new learnings and crucial product releases this quarter.

The week's tiredness was compelling me to cook one main course meal instead of a proper Indian Thali. It needed to be spicy, nutrients-loaded and heavy enough to erase late morning brunch's hunger. While flipping through TV channels, I landed in Delhi's delight, featuring the great "Paranthe-wale-Gali". My hunger reached its zenith and in the thought of paratha's I was literally salivating.

I rushed to the refrigerator, grabbed a cauliflower and some potatoes to make Aloo-Gobi Paratha.

Here goes the Aloo Gobi Paratha recipe:

**Ingredients:**

1. Potato - 1 medium sized
2. Cauliflower - 1/4 big sized
3. Onion - 1 medium sized
4. Garlic - 2 - 3 large cloves, finely chopped
5. Ginger - 1 cm long, finely grated
6. Green chilies - 1 finely chopped
7. Coriander leaves - 1 tbsp finely chopped
8. Red Chili powder - 1/2tsp
9. Cumin Powder - 1/2 tsp
10. Coriander Powder - 1/2 tsp
11. Wheat Flour - 2 cups
12. Salt - according to the taste
13. Ghee/Vegetable Oil
     
     [![Aloo Gobi Paratha Ingredient](/assets/uploads/2015/05/Aloo_Gobi_Paratha_Ingredient.jpg)](/assets/uploads/2015/05/Aloo_Gobi_Paratha_Ingredient.jpg) Aloo Gobi Paratha Ingredients

 **Steps of Preparation:**

**_Steps of Preparation for Dough:_**

1. Add water slowly to wheat flour and start kneading.
2. Knead till the dough is soft.
3. After it's completely kneaded, cover the dough with a wet muslin cloth and let it breathe in the air. This process makes it fluffy.

_**Steps of Preparation for Stuffing:**_

1. Boil the potatoes and mash it properly.
2. Cut the cauliflower into very tiny pieces; in case it looks larger, mash the florets to make it smaller.
3. Add the mashed cauliflower florets to the mashed boiled potatoes.
4. Add the finely chopped onions, green chilies, coriander leaves, finely chopped garlic and finely grated ginger to the mash.
5. Add the spices (cumin powder, coriander powder, chili powder) and salt for flavorings to the mashed mixture and mix it thoroughly.

**_Steps of Preparation for Aloo-Gobi Paratha:_**

1. Make two medium size balls of the wheat dough as shown in the picture.
    
    [![Aloo Gobi Paratha Recipe: Step 01](/assets/uploads/2015/05/Aloo_Gobi_Paratha_Roll_01.jpg)](/assets/uploads/2015/05/Aloo_Gobi_Paratha_Roll_01.jpg) Aloo Gobi Paratha Recipe: Step 01
2. Roll the two small balls into two small circles (as much we do for Puri's).
3. Place one small rolled Puri and add the stuffing of 2 tbsp and cover it up with another Puri shaped roll.
    
    [![Aloo Gobi Paratha Recipe: Step 02](/assets/uploads/2015/05/Aloo_Gobi_Paratha_Roll_02.jpg)](/assets/uploads/2015/05/Aloo_Gobi_Paratha_Roll_02.jpg) Aloo Gobi Paratha Recipe: Step 02
4. Close it firmly, so that it is evenly stuffed and then roll it to round shape.
    
    [![Aloo Gobi Paratha Rolling : Step 03 ](/assets/uploads/2015/05/Aloo_Gobi_Paratha_Roll_03.jpg)](/assets/uploads/2015/05/Aloo_Gobi_Paratha_Roll_03.jpg) Aloo Gobi Paratha Rolling : Step 03
5. Heat a griddle (tawa), place the rolled paratha.
6. Heat the paratha till bubble come. Flip it once and let the other side browns properly.
7. Add 1 tsp of oil and brush it to the top facing side.
8. Flip the paratha and repeat the previous step.
9. Heat the paratha till it is golden brown.
10. Serve the yummy paratha's with curd or top it with butter.
