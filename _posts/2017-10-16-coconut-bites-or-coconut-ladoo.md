For this Diwali I started up with very easy to make Coconut Ladoos.

Here goes the recipe:

#### Ingredients:

1. Unsweetened desiccated Coconut - 2 cups
    
    [![Dessicated Coconut](/assets/uploads/2017/10/DSC_2969.jpg)](/assets/uploads/2017/10/DSC_2969.jpg) Dessicated Coconut
2. Sweetened Condensed Milk - 1/2 cup
    
    [![Sweetened Condensed Milk](/assets/uploads/2017/10/DSC_2971.jpg)](/assets/uploads/2017/10/DSC_2971.jpg) Sweetened Condensed Milk
3. Ground Black Pepper - 1 tsp
4. Ground Cardamom - 1 tsp
    
    [![Black Pepper and Cardamom](/assets/uploads/2017/10/DSC_2970.jpg)](/assets/uploads/2017/10/DSC_2970.jpg) Black Pepper and Cardamom

#### Steps of Preparation:

1. Heat a pan in a medium heat.
2. Saute the Dessicated Coconut till light brown.
    
    [![Dessicated Coconut being Sauted](/assets/uploads/2017/10/DSC_2976.jpg)](/assets/uploads/2017/10/DSC_2976.jpg) Dessicated Coconut being sauted
3. Add the condensed milk and the powdered spices and mix them altogether.
    
    [![Condensed Milk added](/assets/uploads/2017/10/DSC_2982.jpg)](/assets/uploads/2017/10/DSC_2982.jpg) Condensed Milk added
    
    [![](/assets/uploads/2017/10/DSC_2988.jpg)](/assets/uploads/2017/10/DSC_2988.jpg)
    
    [![Addition of dry spices](/assets/uploads/2017/10/DSC_2992.jpg)](/assets/uploads/2017/10/DSC_2992.jpg) Addition of dry spices
4. Cook them till all dough comes together.
    
    [![The final dough](/assets/uploads/2017/10/DSC_2995.jpg)](/assets/uploads/2017/10/DSC_2995.jpg) The final dough
5. Switch off the gas, and let the mixture cool.
6. Pinch off some dough like a size of a lime and roll it to a round shape.
    
    [![Rolling the Ladoos](/assets/uploads/2017/10/DSC_2997.jpg)](/assets/uploads/2017/10/DSC_2997.jpg) Rolling the Ladoos
7. After rolling it into a small sphere, crust it with the dry Dessicated Coconut.
    
    [![Dusting to for a crispy crust](/assets/uploads/2017/10/DSC_3002.jpg)](/assets/uploads/2017/10/DSC_3002.jpg) Dusting to for a crispy crust
