This Adai is bit different from the traditional one and I cooked to clean up my pantry before the next grocery shopping. But the texture came out so good that, I felt if I didn't scribe it and publish it, probably I will be the one who would miss the outcome of the experiment.

Here goes the recipe for Multigrain Adai:

## Ingredients for Multigrain Adai :

1. Whole Green Gram or Moong Beans : 1/4 cup
2. Black Grams or Skinned whole Urad Dal - 1/4 cup
3. Split Pigeon peas or toor Dal - 1/4 cup
4. Channa Dal - 1/4 cup
5. Multi colored Quinoa - 1/2 cup
6. Idli Rice or any variety of short grained par-boiled rice - 1 cup
7. Dried Red chilies - 5
8. Ginger - 2 cm long
9. Curry Leaves - handful
10. Asofoetida or Hing - a pinch
11. Coconut oil - for cooking

## Steps of Preparation for Multigrain Adai:

1. Soak all the grains for overnight.
    
    ![Soaking them overnight](/assets/uploads/2018/09/DSC_6619.jpg) Soaking them overnight
2. Bring all the soaked ingredients and dry it with red chillies, ginger, curry leaves and asofoetida and grind them into a paste.
    
    ![Ingredients for grinding bater](/assets/uploads/2018/09/img_2551.jpg) Ingredients for grinding bater
3. Make sure not to make too much watery, but to a flowing spreadable consistency.
    
    ![Ground Batter](/assets/uploads/2018/09/img_2553.jpg) Ground Batter
4. Heat a griddle, brush some coconut oil to the griddle and spread the batter in round manner.
    
    ![Spreading the laddle full of Batter](/assets/uploads/2018/09/IMG_2560.jpg) Spreading the laddle full of Batter
    
     
    
    ![](/assets/uploads/2018/09/IMG_2566.jpg)
5. Season, few drops of coconut oil on top of the spread batter and let it cook till golden brown on both sides.
    
    ![Adai spread on the griddle](/assets/uploads/2018/09/IMG_2577.jpg) Adai spread on the griddle
    
    ![One sided cooked Adai](/assets/uploads/2018/09/IMG_2580.jpg) One sided cooked Adai
6. Serve it with coconut chutney.
