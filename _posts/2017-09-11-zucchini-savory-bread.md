Here goes the Recipe for Savory Zucchini bread:

[![Who get's this plate?](/assets/uploads/2017/09/DSC_2632.jpg)](/assets/uploads/2017/09/DSC_2632.jpg) Who get's this plate?

### Ingredients:

1. Zucchini - 1 large
2. Jalapeno Hot pepper - 1 large
3. Basil leaves - handful
4. Spring Onions - 3
5. Garlic - 2 large cloves
6. Eggs - 3 large
7. All Purpose Flour - 1 and 1/2 cup
8. Granulated Sugar - 1 tbsp
9. Salt - 1 tsp
10. Baking Powder - 1 and 1/2 tsp
11. Paprika - 1 tsp
12. Olive Oil - 1/3 cup
13. Grated Mozzarella Cheese - a handful
     
     [![Ingredients](/assets/uploads/2017/09/DSC_2556.jpg)](/assets/uploads/2017/09/DSC_2556.jpg) Ingredients:  Basil, Zucchini, Garlic, Spring Onions and Jalapeno Hot Pepper

### Steps of Preparation:

1. Preheat the oven to 350 F or 180 deg Celsius.
2. Grate the Zucchini and Garlic and strain the excess water out of it.
    
    [![Grated Zucchini](/assets/uploads/2017/09/DSC_2561.jpg)](/assets/uploads/2017/09/DSC_2561.jpg) Grated Zucchini
    
    [![Grating the Garlic Cloves](/assets/uploads/2017/09/DSC_2565.jpg)](/assets/uploads/2017/09/DSC_2565.jpg) Grating the Garlic Cloves
3. Chop the basil leaves and spring onions.
    
    [![Chopping the Basil Leaves](/assets/uploads/2017/09/DSC_2571.jpg)](/assets/uploads/2017/09/DSC_2571.jpg) Chopping the Basil Leaves
    
    [![Chopping the Spring Onions](/assets/uploads/2017/09/DSC_2578.jpg)](/assets/uploads/2017/09/DSC_2578.jpg) Chopping the Spring Onions
4. Chop the Jalapeno and mix it with the rest all vegetables and herbs. [![](/assets/uploads/2017/09/DSC_2588.jpg)](/assets/uploads/2017/09/DSC_2588.jpg)
    
    [![Little hands playing with the veggies](/assets/uploads/2017/09/DSC_2594.jpg)](/assets/uploads/2017/09/DSC_2594.jpg) Little hands playing with the veggies
5. Beat the three eggs and add to the veggies.
    
    [![Eggs](/assets/uploads/2017/09/DSC_2591.jpg)](/assets/uploads/2017/09/DSC_2591.jpg) Eggs
    
    [![](/assets/uploads/2017/09/DSC_2596.jpg)](/assets/uploads/2017/09/DSC_2596.jpg)
6. Sieve the dry ingredients and add the beaten egg to the mixture and fold gently the veggie mixture.
7. Add the olive oil to the batter.
    
    [![The Batter](/assets/uploads/2017/09/DSC_2600.jpg)](/assets/uploads/2017/09/DSC_2600.jpg) The Batter
8. Line the bread mold with a parchment paper and pour in the batter.
9. Sprinkle the cheese for the extra delicious flavor and texture.
    
    [![Mold ready to go in the Oven](/assets/uploads/2017/09/DSC_2603.jpg)](/assets/uploads/2017/09/DSC_2603.jpg) Mold ready to go in the Oven
10. Bake it for 30-35 mins.
     
     [![Straight out from oven](/assets/uploads/2017/09/DSC_2616.jpg)](/assets/uploads/2017/09/DSC_2616.jpg) Straight out from oven

Once done serve it hot or cold and with a cup of coffee. It's a true delight.
