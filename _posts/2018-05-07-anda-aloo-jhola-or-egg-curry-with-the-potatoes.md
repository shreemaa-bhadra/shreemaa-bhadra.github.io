Egg and Potatoes Served

Speaking about this recipe, it's a thin curry made with Egg and Potatoes tossed in the Onions, Ginger-Garlic and Tomato gravy and cooked with a sprinkle of Garam Masala. This is served with a bowl of hot rice or rotis. I can eat it umpteen times without any decline in interest. So here goes one more easy peasy recipe for your everyday meal.

![Potatoes in the gravy](/assets/uploads/2018/05/DSC_4569.jpg)

Potatoes in the gravy

### Ingredients:

1. Large Eggs - 5
2. Medium sized Potatoes - 3
3. Tomato - 1 medium sized, finely chopped
4. Onion - 1 medium sized, finely chopped
5. Garlic Cloves - 2 large
6. Ginger - 1/2 inch
7. Cumin seeds - 1 tsp
8. Coriander seeds  - 1 tsp
9. Red chilies - 3
10. Bay Leaves - 1
11. Cinnamon Stick - 1
12. Cardamom - 2 pods
13. Mustard Oil - 2 tbsp
14. Turmeric Powder - 1 tsp
15. Salt - according to taste
16. Coriander leaves - for garnishing
17. Green Chilies - 2, slit

### Steps of Preparation:

1. Firstly, boil the Eggs and Potatoes in medium high for about 20 mins, until the eggs are hard boiled and potatoes are cooked firmly.
   
  ![Boiling the Eggs and Poatoes](/assets/uploads/2018/05/DSC_4465.jpg)

Boiling the Eggs and Poatoes
2. In the meantime, make a coarse ground paste of coriander seeds, cumin seeds, red chilies, ginger and garlic.
   
  ![Ingredients for Gravy](/assets/uploads/2018/05/DSC_4466.jpg)

Ingredients for Gravy
   
  ![Ground Paste](/assets/uploads/2018/05/DSC_4481.jpg)

Ground Paste
3. Once Egg and Potatoes are cooled, peel off the egg and potatoes and cut the potatoes to two equal halves.
   
  ![Boiled Eggs and Potatoes](/assets/uploads/2018/05/DSC_4470.jpg)

Boiled Eggs and Potatoes
4. Heat a wide bottomed vessel, and add mustard oil to it. Gently fry the boiled eggs and potatoes in separate batches.
   
  ![Adding the Mustard Oil](/assets/uploads/2018/05/DSC_4475.jpg)

Adding the Mustard Oil
   
  ![Shallow Frying the Boiled Eggs](/assets/uploads/2018/05/DSC_4486.jpg)

Shallow Frying the Boiled Eggs
   
  ![Frying the Potatoes](/assets/uploads/2018/05/DSC_4489.jpg)

Frying the Potatoes
5. Once done, to the same oil, add the whole spices like Bay Leaves, Cinnamon and Cardamom and gently toss it for a minute.
   
  ![Adding the whole spices Bay Leaves, Cardamom and cinnamon](/assets/uploads/2018/05/DSC_4492.jpg)

Adding the whole spices Bay Leaves, Cardamom and cinnamon
6. Add the finely chopped onions to the whole spices and cook it till softened and the raw smell goes off.
   
  ![Adding Onions](/assets/uploads/2018/05/DSC_4497.jpg)

Adding Onions
   
  ![Cooked Onions](/assets/uploads/2018/05/DSC_4501.jpg)

Cooked Onions
7. Next, when the onions are cooked, add the coarsely ground paste and cook it till the oil oozes out of the mixture.
   
  ![Adding the Ground Paste](/assets/uploads/2018/05/DSC_4504.jpg)

Adding the Ground Paste
   
  ![Cooking the ground paste](/assets/uploads/2018/05/DSC_4508.jpg)

Cooking the ground paste
8. Then add the turmeric and salt and saute.!["<yoastmark](/assets/uploads/2018/05/DSC_4511.jpg)
   
  ![Adding Salt](/assets/uploads/2018/05/DSC_4517.jpg)

Adding Salt
9. The tomatoes are added later to the mixture and cooked till the oil oozes out.
   
  ![Adding Tomatoes](/assets/uploads/2018/05/DSC_4516.jpg)

Adding Tomatoes
   
  ![The cooked Masala](/assets/uploads/2018/05/DSC_4530.jpg)

The cooked Masala
10. 2 cups of water is further added and brought to a boil. Then add the potatoes and egg and cook it for 2 mins. I didn't let it shimmer a lot as I wanted a very thin gravy.
   
  ![Adding the water to the Masala](/assets/uploads/2018/05/DSC_4535.jpg)

Adding the water to the Masala
   
  !["<yoastmark](/assets/uploads/2018/05/DSC_4540.jpg)
11. Finally, add the coriander leaves and green chilies.
   
  ![Adding the green chilies and Coriander Leaves](/assets/uploads/2018/05/DSC_4543.jpg)

Adding the green chilies and Coriander Leaves
