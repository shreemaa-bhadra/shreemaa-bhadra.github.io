The recipe of Peas Paratha or Mutter Paratha goes like this:

**Ingredients:**

**For making stuffing:**

1. Green Peas - 300 gms - 400 gms
2. Garlic - 5-6 cloves
3. Onion - 2 medium sized finely chopped
4. Ginger - 2.5 inch long
5. Cumin Powder - 2 tsp
6. Coriander Powder - 2 tsp
7. Red chili Powder - 2 tsp
8. Green Chillies - 3 -4
9. Vegetable Oil - 2 tbsp
10. Besan or Gram Flour - 2 tbsp

**For making Paratha:**

1. Wheat Flour - 2cups
2. Water - for kneading
3.  Vegetable Oil - for shallow frying

 **Steps of Preparation of Dough:**

1. Add water slowly to wheat flour and start kneading.
2. Knead till the dough is soft.
3. After it's completely kneaded, cover the dough with a wet muslin cloth and let it breathe in the air. This process makes it fluffy.

**Steps of Preparation of Stuffing:**

1. Peel the Green Peas and clean it and pat dry.
2. In a mixer grinder, coarsely grind the Green Peas, garlic cloves, green chilies and ginger.
3. [![Mutter Paratha \_ SA21](/assets/uploads/2016/03/DSC_6150.jpg)](/assets/uploads/2016/03/DSC_6150.jpg) Mutter Paratha \_ SA21
    
    [![Mutter Paratha\_SA22](/assets/uploads/2016/03/DSC_6157.jpg)](/assets/uploads/2016/03/DSC_6157.jpg) Mutter Paratha\_SA22
    
    [![Mutter Paratha \_ SA23](/assets/uploads/2016/03/DSC_6168.jpg)](/assets/uploads/2016/03/DSC_6168.jpg) Mutter Paratha \_ SA23
4. In a wide bottomed base Pan or wok or Kadhai, add 2 tbsp of vegetable oil and let it heat for 2 mins. After the oil is hot, add the cumin seeds and let it tamper in the hot oil.
    
    [![Mutter Paratha \_ SA3](/assets/uploads/2016/03/DSC_6172.jpg)](/assets/uploads/2016/03/DSC_6172.jpg) Mutter Paratha \_ SA3
5. Saute the cumin seeds, then add the Onion and fry till its golden brown.
    
    [![Mutter Paratha \_ SA41](/assets/uploads/2016/03/DSC_6163.jpg)](/assets/uploads/2016/03/DSC_6163.jpg) Mutter Paratha \_ SA41
    
    [![Mutter Paratha \_ SA42](/assets/uploads/2016/03/DSC_6178.jpg)](/assets/uploads/2016/03/DSC_6178.jpg) Mutter Paratha \_ SA42
6. To the fried Onions add the coarsely ground mutter paste.
    
    [![Mutter Paratha \_ SA5](/assets/uploads/2016/03/DSC_6180.jpg)](/assets/uploads/2016/03/DSC_6180.jpg) Mutter Paratha \_ SA5
7. Saute it till the raw flavors of ginger, garlic dies off and the mutter is cooked.
8. Add 1 tsp of coriander powder, 1 tsp of cumin powder, 1 tsp of red chili powder and salt according to the taste.
    
    [![Mutter Paratha \_ SA71](/assets/uploads/2016/03/DSC_6187.jpg)](/assets/uploads/2016/03/DSC_6187.jpg) Mutter Paratha \_ SA71
    
    [![Mutter Paratha \_ SA72](/assets/uploads/2016/03/DSC_6194.jpg)](/assets/uploads/2016/03/DSC_6194.jpg) Mutter Paratha \_ SA72
9. To the cooked paste, add 2 tbsp of gram flour or Besan. We use the gram flour to help with binding the stuffing and make it thicker.
    
    [![Mutter Paratha\_ SA8](/assets/uploads/2016/03/DSC_6197.jpg)](/assets/uploads/2016/03/DSC_6197.jpg) Mutter Paratha\_ SA8
10. After the stuffing is properly cooked in the shown consistency. Keep the stuffing aside and let it cool in the room temperature.
     
     [![Mutter Paratha \_ SA9](/assets/uploads/2016/03/DSC_6204.jpg)](/assets/uploads/2016/03/DSC_6204.jpg) Mutter Paratha \_ SA9

**Steps of Preparation of Mutter Paratha:**

1. Make two medium size balls of the wheat dough as shown in the picture.
    
    [![Mutter Paratha \_ SB1](/assets/uploads/2016/03/DSC_6210.jpg)](/assets/uploads/2016/03/DSC_6210.jpg) Mutter Paratha \_ SB1
2. Roll the two small balls into small circles apparently the size of the Puri.
    
    [![Mutter Paratha \_ SB2](/assets/uploads/2016/03/DSC_6211.jpg)](/assets/uploads/2016/03/DSC_6211.jpg) Mutter Paratha \_ SB2
3. Place one small rolled Puri and add the stuffing of 2 tbsp and cover it up with another rolled Puri.
    
    [![Mutter Paratha \_ SB3](/assets/uploads/2016/03/DSC_6215.jpg)](/assets/uploads/2016/03/DSC_6215.jpg) Mutter Paratha \_ SB3
4. Close it firmly, so that it is evenly stuffed and then roll it to the round shape.
    
    [![Mutter Paratha \_ SB41](/assets/uploads/2016/03/DSC_6217.jpg)](/assets/uploads/2016/03/DSC_6217.jpg) Mutter Paratha \_ SB41
    
    [![Mutter Paratha \_ SB42](/assets/uploads/2016/03/DSC_6220.jpg)](/assets/uploads/2016/03/DSC_6220.jpg) Mutter Paratha \_ SB42
5. Heat a griddle (tawa), place the rolled Paratha.
    
    [![Mutter Paratha\_ SB5](/assets/uploads/2016/03/DSC_6224.jpg)](/assets/uploads/2016/03/DSC_6224.jpg) Mutter Paratha\_ SB5
    
6. Heat the Paratha till the bubble come. Flip it once and let the other side browns properly.
7. Add 1 tsp of oil and brush it to the top facing side.
    
    [![Mutter Paratha \_ SB7](/assets/uploads/2016/03/DSC_6231.jpg)](/assets/uploads/2016/03/DSC_6231.jpg) Mutter Paratha \_ SB7
8. Flip the Paratha and repeat the previous step.

Heat the Paratha till it's golden brown on both the sides.
