> Khiri or Kheer is the sweetened rice pudding, where the rice is slow cooked with milk, sweetened with Sugar, flavored with cardamom and garnished with nuts and dry fruits .

Here goes the simple yet classic recipe of Kheer or Khiri:

### **Ingredients of Khiri or Rice Kheer:**

-  Fragrant rice - 1/4 cup, I used Kali Jeera variety, basmati can be used as well
- Whole milk or Full fat milk - 1/2 gallon (1.8 litres)
- Sugar - 1 cup
- Sweetened Condensed milk - 1/2 cup
- Cardamom pods - 3-4, crushed
- Ghee or Clarified Butter - 4 tbsp
- Cashews, Pistachios and Raisins for garnish

### **Steps of preparation for Khiri or Rice Kheer:**

- Take a deep bottomed vessel.
- Heat the vessel with 2 tbsp of clarified butter or ghee.
- Wash and strain the rice. Add the rice to the ghee and fry it for some time, until the nutty flavor of the rice comes.
- Add one cup of water and cover it and cook until its 75% done.
- Then add the milk and stir.
- Keep stirring the milk for 20-25 mins in a medium flame, until the boil comes.
- Add the sugar and keep stirring for around more 30-35 mins.
- Then add the condensed milk and stir for around 20 mins.
- After the Khiri is made, switch off the flame.
- Take a small frying pan, add more 2 tbsp of ghee and add the nuts and dry fruits to toast it.
- Then add the nuts and dry fruits for garnish.

Serve the yummy Khiri or Rice Kheer in this [festive season](https://cookspan.com/tag/festival/) as a mandatory dessert. This classic dessert never goes wrong for the festive season.
